Biography

I am a 3rd year PhD student at the Embedded and Networked Systems groups at the Delft University of Technology. My advisor is Professor Marco Zuniga.

My research interests are in the areas of visible light communication, wireless sensing, pervasive and mobile computing. I am also interested in (though not actively working on) RF sensing and biomedical systems and applications. I am affiliated with the European Training Network in Low-Energy Visible Light IoT Systems (ENLIGHT’EM) project.
